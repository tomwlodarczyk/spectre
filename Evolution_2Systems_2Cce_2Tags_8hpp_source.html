24 /// \brief Bondi parameter \f$H = \partial_u J\f$.
25 /// \note The notation in the literature is not consistent regarding this
26 /// quantity, or whether it is denoted by an \f$H\f$ at all. The SpECTRE CCE
27 /// module consistently uses it to describe the (retarded) partial time
28 /// derivative of \f$J\f$ at fixed compactified radius \f$y\f$ (to be contrasted
29 /// with the physical Bondi radius, which is not directly used for numerical
30 /// grids).

34 };

143 /// the spin weight, It is assumed that the linear factor plays the role of
