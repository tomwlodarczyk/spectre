31 /*!
32  * \brief Analytic initial data for axially symmetric Bondi-Hoyle accretion.
33  *
34  * In the context of studying Bondi-Hoyle accretion, i.e. non-spherical
35  * accretion on to a Kerr black hole moving relative to a gas cloud, this class
36  * implements the method proposed by Font & Ibáñez (1998) \ref font_98 "[1]" to
37  * initialize the GRMHD variables. The fluid quantities are initialized with
38  * their (constant) values far from the black hole, e.g.
39  * \f$\rho = \rho_\infty\f$. Here we assume a
40  * polytropic equation of state, so only the rest mass density, as well as the
41  * polytropic constant and the polytropic exponent, are provided as inputs.
42  * The spatial velocity is initialized using a field that ensures that the
43  * injected gas reproduces a continuous parallel wind at large distances.
44  * The direction of this flow is chosen to be along the black hole spin.
45  * In Kerr (or "spherical Kerr-Schild", see gr::KerrSchildCoords) coordinates,
46  *
47  * \f{align*}
48  * v^r &= \frac{1}{\sqrt{\gamma_{rr}}}v_\infty \cos\theta\\
49  * v^\theta &= -\frac{1}{\sqrt{\gamma_{\theta\theta}}}v_\infty \sin\theta\\
50  * v^\phi &= 0.
51  * \f}
52  *
53  * where \f$\gamma_{ij} = g_{ij}\f$ is the spatial metric, and \f$v_\infty\f$
54  * is the flow speed far from the black hole. Note that
55  * \f$v_\infty^2 = v_i v^i\f$. Finally, following the work by
56  * Penner (2011) \ref penner_11 "[2]",
57  * the magnetic field is initialized using Wald's solution to Maxwell's
58  * equations in Kerr black hole spacetime. In Kerr ("spherical
59  * Kerr-Schild") coordinates, the spatial components of the Faraday tensor read
60  *
61  * \f{align*}
62  * F_{r\theta} &= a B_0 \left[1 + \frac{2Mr}{\Sigma^2}(r^2 - a^2)\right]
63  * \sin\theta\cos\theta\\
64  * F_{\theta\phi} &= B_0\left[\Delta +
65  * \frac{2Mr}{\Sigma^2}(r^4 - a^4)\right]\sin\theta\cos\theta\\
66  * F_{\phi r} &= - B_0\left[r + \frac{M a^2}{\Sigma^2}
67  * (r^2 - a^2\cos^2\theta)(1 + \cos^2\theta)\right]\sin^2\theta.
68  * \f}
69  *
70  * where \f$\Sigma = r^2 + a^2\cos^2\theta\f$ and \f$\Delta = r^2 - 2Mr +
71  * a^2\f$. The associated Eulerian magnetic field is
72  *
73  * \f{align*}
74  * B^r = \frac{F_{\theta\phi}}{\sqrt\gamma},\quad
75  * B^\theta = \frac{F_{\phi r}}{\sqrt\gamma},\quad
76  * B^\phi = \frac{F_{r\theta}}{\sqrt\gamma}.
77  * \f}
78  *
79  * where \f$\gamma = \text{det}(\gamma_{ij})\f$. Wald's solution reproduces a
80  * uniform magnetic field far from the black hole.
81  *
82  * \anchor font_98 [1] J.A. Font & J.M. Ibáñez, ApJ
83  * [494 (1998) 297](http://esoads.eso.org/abs/1998ApJ...494..297F)
84  *
85  * \anchor penner_11 [2] A.J. Penner, MNRAS
86  * [414 (2011) 1467](http://cdsads.u-strasbg.fr/abs/2011MNRAS.414.1467P)
87  */
