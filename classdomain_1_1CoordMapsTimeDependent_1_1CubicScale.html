Detailed Description

CubicScale map defined by \(x = a(t)*\xi+(b(t)-a(t))\xi^3/X^2\), where \(X\) is the outer boundary.

The map scales the coordinates \(\xi\) near the center by a factor \(a(t)\), while the coordinates near the outer boundary \(X\), are scaled by a factor \(b(t)\). Here \(a(t)\) and \(b(t)\) are FunctionsOfTime.

Currently, only a 1-D implementation.
