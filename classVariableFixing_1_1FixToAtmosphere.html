Detailed Description

template<size_t ThermodynamicDim>
class VariableFixing::FixToAtmosphere< ThermodynamicDim >

Fix the primitive variables to an atmosphere in low density regions.

If the rest mass density is below \(\rho_{\textrm{cutoff}}\) (DensityCutoff), it is set to \(\rho_{\textrm{atm}}\) (DensityOfAtmosphere), and the pressure, specific internal energy (for one-dimensional equations of state), and specific enthalpy are adjusted to satisfy the equation of state. For a two-dimensional equation of state, the specific internal energy is set to zero. In addition, the spatial velocity is set to zero, and the Lorentz factor is set to one.
